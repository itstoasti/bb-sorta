Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Boba Sort mobile application (the "Service") created by deanfieldz (the "Developer") for mobile platforms. This policy governs how we collect, store, and utilize data within the Service.

1. Collection of Personal Data

The Service does not collect, request, or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or geographic location. You can play all levels of the game anonymously without registration.

2. Local Device Storage

To preserve your game progress, the Service uses local device storage (AsyncStorage). This stores only gameplay progression states, such as:

  • Your unlocked level index (1-100)
  • Your earned coin balance
  • Your customized name for your Boba Cafe
  • Your unlocked and equipped cosmetics (cup models, straws, cafe decorations)
  • Your daily reward claim dates

This data remains entirely on your local device and is not synchronized to any developer-owned web servers.

3. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

The Service utilizes the **RevenueCat** platform to process safe in-app purchases (such as "Remove Ads Forever") and check entitlement validation state. All transactional processing is managed directly by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store checkout layers. We do not collect or store your billing coordinates or credit card information.

4. Advertising Networks (Google AdMob)

The Service displays third-party video, static, and interstitial ads processed via the **Google AdMob SDK**. These networks may gather temporary, non-personal device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Your device type, screen size, and OS version
  • Your device's Advertising Identifier (Google Ad ID or iOS IDFA)

If you purchase the "Remove Ads Forever" upgrade, all AdMob requests are disabled, preventing any tracking actions.

5. Children's Privacy

Our puzzle Service is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly compile personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has transmitted any identifying details to us, please notify us immediately so we can remove the data.

6. Consent and Updates

By installing and using the Service, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy periodically. Your continued use following updates signifies your approval of the revised terms.

7. Contact Details

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, feel free to contact the developer directly at deanfieldz@gmail.com.
